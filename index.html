Scholar | Reporter | Journalist

Charlotte Payne is a Presidential Scholarship recipient and Dean's List student at the University Of Miami, School of Communication, Class of 2020. She is majoring in Broadcast Journalism and minoring in Sports Administration. She previously studied at Loyola Marymount University and New York University before transferring to the University of Miami. She was a Dean's List scholar at both LMU and NYU. Charlotte is a member of the National Broadcasting Society. At the University of Miami she has been an Executive Producer of NewsBreak, a Host, Anchor and Reporter for SportsDesk, NewsBreak, and a segment Host on UMTV Pulse.
